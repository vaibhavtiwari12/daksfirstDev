Meet Our Team

At Daksfirst

Sumit Kanthed

Managing Partner

"Sumit comes with capital markets expertise with coverage extending to Europe and the Middle East. He comes with 14+ years’ experience in structured credit, derivatives, corporate finance and asset management with a focus on emerging markets accompanied by a broad institutional network. He worked at JP Morgan and a Buy side boutique PE, a private equity firm in London He is also an investor in Daksfirst and Co-CEO of Maystone Capital a UK focussed merchant bank. He holds a Bachelor and Masters in engineering from IIT Bombay, an MBA from IIM Lucknow and CFA and FRM certifications"

Kartik Krishnaswamy

Investor Relations

"Kartik started his career in management consulting in North America and Europe serving a diverse corporate client base for 8 years. In the last decade he has focussed on the asset management business for South Asia at Barclays Investment Management and at Crossbridge Capital, a London based asset management firm. Currently, he runs Maystone Capital as a Co-CEO alongside Sumit covering clients in Developed markets. He holds a BS in Industrial Engg, MS in Applied Mathematics and an MBA from University of Chicago"

Amit Kanthed

Vice President, Investment Team
